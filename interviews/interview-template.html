Overview

What was your application process like? Did you get a recruiter cold message, apply online, get a referral? That context matters and makes the story more real.

The Process

01
Recruiter Phone Screen
30 min · Phone · HR/Recruiter
Describe what happened in this round. What was the tone? What broad topics came up? How long did it last? Was it behavioral, technical, or a mix?
02
Technical Screen
60 min · Zoom · Security Engineer
What was the technical format? Live coding? Whiteboard? Verbal Q&A? Walk through a CVE? Describe the structure and the general topics covered.
03
Final Round — Panel
90 min · Zoom · 3 interviewers
Who was in the room (roles, not names)? What did each person focus on? How did the vibe feel?

What I'd Do Differently

Write your honest retrospective here. What did you miss? What technical gaps did this interview expose? What would you prepare differently if you could go back?

Specifics are better than vague regrets. Instead of "I'd study more," say "I'd drill OWASP Top 10 with hands-on PortSwigger labs instead of just reading the list."

Key Takeaways

// lessons learned

  • Another concrete takeaway. What did this interview teach you about the role or company?
  • A technical gap this process exposed and how you addressed it afterward.
  • Something about the interview format or process itself that surprised you.
